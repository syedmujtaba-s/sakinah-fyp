Sakinah v1.0.1 — hotfix release.

Fixed:
- White screen on app launch after install. Two root causes addressed:
  1. Native Android splash now shows Sakinah logo instead of pure white
     while the Flutter engine boots (could take 2-4s on lower-end phones).
  2. Firebase + notification service init now have hard timeouts (8s / 5s).
     If either hangs on flaky network or missing Play Services, the app
     still boots into the splash screen instead of staying blank forever.

If you had the previous v1.0.0 build that showed white screen — just
update and reopen. You should now see the green Sakinah logo on launch.

Anything else weird, hit the "Send Feedback" screen and it'll show up
in the admin panel.
