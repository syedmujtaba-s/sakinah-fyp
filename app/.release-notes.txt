Sakinah v1.0.2 — UX polish + journal management.

What's new:

1. Off-topic detection
   When you type something that isn't an emotional reflection (a factual
   question, a code snippet, random chat), the app now gently redirects
   you with example prompts instead of forcing a Seerah story onto your
   non-emotional input. The bad entry is also NOT saved to your history.

2. Delete journal entries
   Long-press / 3-dot menu on any journal in History → Delete. A
   confirmation dialog gives you the option to also wipe bookmarks and
   habits saved from that story (off by default — your saves are
   protected unless you opt in).

3. Removed white-screen residue
   v1.0.1's launch fix carries over: branded splash + resilient init.

If anything feels off, hit the Send Feedback screen and it'll land in
the admin panel.
