Sakinah v1.0.5 — fully customisable reminders.

New:

1. Tap the time chip on any reminder (Journal Reflection, Morning Adhkar,
   Evening Adhkar, Habit Check-in) to pick your own time. Native Android
   time picker, supports both 12/24-hour formats.

2. Per-day-of-week control. Each reminder shows seven small chips
   (M T W T F S S). Tap to toggle that day on or off — e.g. skip Friday,
   or run a habit reminder only on weekends. At least one day must stay
   selected.

3. Both time and day preferences sync to your account so they survive
   reinstalls and follow you to a new device.

How it works:
- Time chip → tap → pick → notification re-schedules immediately.
- Day chip → tap → that day's alarm is added or cancelled. Other days
  are unaffected (so you can change days without losing the reminder
  on the rest of the week).
- Defaults still 8 PM / 6 AM / 5 PM / 9 PM (every day) if you don't
  customise.

Carries forward:
- v1.0.4 notification reliability fixes (icon, exact alarms, channel,
  permission handling, test button).

Existing users upgrading from v1.0.4: your old daily-style alarms are
automatically cancelled on first launch of v1.0.5 — no duplicate
notifications.
